WHO ARE WE

Children of Tomorrow is an empowerment big brother and mentorship program. We bring together underserved children from slams neighborhoods and talented and motivated mentors through various fields of sports activities. Our goal is to provide an alternative path for these children and hopefully give them the opportunity to grow as professionals in the field they choose.

OUR STORY

The program started as a big brother project that was initiated by Shaked Izrael (the founder) in 2017, At the time Shaked was a young Psychology student. At one occasion Shaked was exposed to the phenomena of groups of Ethiopians children that get together almost every night, drinking, smoking and looking for troubles trying to dissolve their boredom. Shaked decided to start a program that would provide these children with an alternative path, a one that would not only provide them with value and deep and meaningful connections but would also give them the opportunity to become pro's in a field of sport of their choice. Today, two years into the future, "Children Of Tomorrow" provides every child with a mentor, professional guidance twice a week, all the necessary equipment, logistics, transportation and more. Every child is meticulously chosen and we make sure to choose those who need it the most.

OUR GOALS

Providing an alternative and positive path for youth at risk of delinquent behavior

Creating a sustainable and meaningful mentorship relationship that would last!

Providing the children with the opportunity to become pro's in a field of sport of their choice.
